Graduation Project · 2026
Motivation
Problem

Bufferbloat: A single TCP flow causes 693 ms queue delay on a 20 Mbit/s WAN connection — making gaming and VoIP unusable.

Static QoS: Even modern AQMs like CAKE rely on DSCP tags; ISPs strip these tags, causing all traffic to fall into the same queue.

Fixed bandwidth: WAN speed limit is configured once during setup; it cannot react to real-time congestion signals.

Approach
Solution
1

Biological Inspiration: Adaptive nutrient distribution strategy of fungal mycelium networks → reflexive network control

2

6-Class Device Persona: VOIP, GAMING, VIDEO, STREAMING, BULK, TORRENT — On-chip <1 μs decision without DPI or ML

🎮 Gaming 📺 Streaming 📞 VoIP 📦 Bulk 🌊 Torrent ❓ Unknown
3

12-Class Flow Taxonomy: 3-signal weighted voting combining passive DNS, target port hints, and behavioral fingerprinting

4

Zero Flash Writes: All state is kept in RAM — no damage to NAND flash lifespan (live tested on Xiaomi AX3000T)
